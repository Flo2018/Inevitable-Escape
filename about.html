Who Am I?

I come from a family where your business is everyone else's business. Where we share our things equally and don't complain. My brother and I argue constantly but that's okay, I love him anyway. I am the only girl in my family who is 17 years old so I had to learn how to be mature and interact with older kids. I am a competitive person, but you won't know until I get the winning results. I absolutely love the color pink. I get really invested in the T.V. shows that I watch, so much that it feels like I inserted myself into the story. First impressions really stick with me whether it is good or bad, I will remember the impression you made with me. I am a very friendly person. I enjoy games from Assasin's Creed to Game of War on the phone.
